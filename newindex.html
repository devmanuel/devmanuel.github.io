I work in the mathematical foundations of computer science, focusing on automata, logics, algebra, and games.

My research interests include automata over infinite alphabets, logical definability, semigroup methods, formal language theory, and quantitative models of computation.

Research Interests

  • Automata theory and formal languages
  • Algebraic methods in computation
  • First-order and fixpoint logics
  • Cost functions and quantitative automata
  • Semigroups and language varieties
